Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount to us. This policy outlines how Tab Limiter handles information when you use our browser extension and related services.

01. Introduction

Tab Limiter is designed to be a "Privacy-First" tool. We believe that your browsing habits are your business, not ours. Our core philosophy is minimal data footprint. Most of our processing happens locally on your machine, and we never sell your data to third parties.

02. Information We Collect

We differentiate between "Personal Data" and "Usage Data". Here is exactly what we see:

  • check_circle
    Configuration Settings: We store your preferred tab limits and whitelist settings. These are stored locally via Chrome's storage API.
  • check_circle
    Extension Telemetry: To improve our service, we collect anonymous usage statistics such as "total tabs closed" and "extension version".
  • cancel
    Browsing History: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit your URL history or the content of the pages you visit.

03. Browser Permissions

To function correctly, Tab Limiter requires the following permissions from your browser:

tabs "tabs"

Used to count active tabs and close them when the limit is exceeded.

storage "storage"

Used to save your custom settings and whitelist sync across your devices.

04. Data Security

While most data is local, any data that is synchronized (via your Google Account) is encrypted in transit and at rest. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data.
